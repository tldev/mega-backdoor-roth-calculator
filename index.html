Assumptions & fine print. Per-check gross pay is salary ÷ 26 (bi-weekly) or ÷ 24 (semi-monthly) - raises will shift the percentages, so re-check after any comp change. Election changes usually take a pay period or two to apply; make them a few days before payday. A year-end true-up is typically deposited early the following year but generally counts toward this year's 415(c) limit. Compensation above the 401(a)(17) cap can't be matched or deferred against.
